# Insurance-Data-Analysis-Power-BI-Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
As data continues to reshape the insurance industry, the ability to turn policyholder patterns into strategic decisions is critical. I developed this end-to-end analytics project to simulate a real-world business intelligence environment, managing a dataset of 10,000+ policy records.

This repository showcases my ability to handle large datasets, perform ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) processes, and build high-level visualizations that help stakeholders minimize risk and maximize premium revenue.

## 💡 The Business Problem
Insurance providers often struggle with high claim-to-premium ratios and delayed claim processing. I designed this dashboard to solve three specific needs:

Revenue Visibility: Identifying which policy types (e.g., Health vs. Auto) are the most profitable.

Claim Lifecycle Monitoring: Providing a real-time snapshot of Settled vs. Rejected claims to improve operational transparency.

Risk Profiling: Segmenting customers by age and gender to predict future coverage demands.

## 🛠️ Technical Implementation
Data Foundation: Processed InsuranceData.csv (10,000+ rows) ensuring data integrity across policy dates and financial figures.

Analytics Tool: Power BI Desktop.

Core Skills Demonstrated:

DAX Modeling: Created custom measures to calculate average premiums, claim success rates, and loss ratios.

Interactive UX: Implemented dynamic filters for "Policy Type" and "Claim Status" to allow users to drill down into specific data segments.

Data Storytelling: Designed a visual hierarchy that moves from high-level KPIs to granular customer insights.
